# 📊 Superstore Sales Analytics Dashboard (Excel)

## 🔍 Overview
This project showcases my ability to **analyze business data, extract insights, and communicate results through dashboards** using Microsoft Excel. The analysis is based on the *Sample Superstore* dataset and focuses on understanding sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and regional trends in a U.S. retail context.

The goal of this project is to demonstrate **core data analyst skills** such as data preparation, KPI definition, trend analysis, and insight storytelling — all essential for **data analyst internships**.

---

## 🗂 Dataset Summary
- **Domain:** Retail / Office Supplies  
- **Time Period:** 2016–2019  
- **Geography:** United States  
- **Granularity:** Order-level transactional data  

**Key Features:**
- Orders, shipping modes, and delivery times  
- Customers and segments (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)  
- Regions, states, and cities  
- Product categories and sub-categories  
- Sales, profit, discounts, and returns  

---

## 📁 Sheet Structure

### Orders
Main transactional table used for all analysis.
- Sales, profit, discount, quantity
- Order dates and shipping details
- Customer, region, and product attributes
- Return indicator

### People
Lookup table mapping **sales managers to regions**, enabling regional accountability analysis.

### Returns
Tracks returned orders, used to analyze:
- Return rates
- Impact of returns on profitability

### Supporting / Attribute Tables
Used for:
- Data normalization
- Pivot table efficiency
- Clean dashboard calculations

### Dashboard
An interactive Excel dashboard summarizing key business performance metrics.

---

## 📈 Key Metrics (KPIs)
- Total Sales  
- Total Profit  
- Profit Ratio  
- Average Discount  

These KPIs allow quick evaluation of overall business health.

---

## 📊 Analysis & Insights

### 📦 Product & Category Analysis
- Office Supplies lead in sales volume  
- Technology products generate the highest profit margins  
- Furniture contains many loss-making products due to high discounts  

**Insight:** Pricing and discount strategies should be reviewed for furniture items.

---

### 🌎 Regional Performance
- West and East regions dominate total sales  
- Central and South regions show relatively better profit efficiency  
- City-level analysis highlights large urban markets like Los Angeles and New York  

**Insight:** High sales regions do not always mean high profitability.

---

### 👥 Customer Segments
- Consumer segment drives the highest order volume  
- Corporate segment provides higher profit per order  
- Home Office customers receive higher discounts and show higher return rates  

**Insight:** Corporate customers are more profitable despite lower volume.

---

### 💸 Discount vs Profit
- Strong negative correlation between high discounts and profit  
- Several loss-leader products identified  

**Insight:** Excessive discounting directly reduces profitability.

---

### 📆 Time-Series Trends
- Clear Q4 (Oct–Dec) sales spikes each year  
- Seasonal purchasing behavior visible  

**Insight:** Data supports demand forecasting and inventory planning.

---

### 🔁 Returns Analysis
- Higher return frequency in Office Supplies and Home Office  
- Returned orders are often unprofitable  

**Insight:** Return-heavy products and segments require policy review.
